ANTIHUMAN: Subject 1


"The thing to finally bring humanity to its knees hadn't been climate change nor nuclear war, it had been far simpler than that. It all started with a single cough.

Project Clean Up was one of necessity: to give proper burial to the many lives lost and reclaim land from the sickness.

Akim Valenka, a strapping young man in his late 20s, had finally graduated from training and joined Clean Up's ranks as a green scout. He could finally fulfill his dream to help reclaim his family's hometown from the horror and death that permeated the land, and perhaps one day, move back to their roots. He may have been born in the Golden City, but many nights in his youth listening to his father's stories had lit something deep inside his chest.

One thing he hadn't expected however... was the discovery that people still lived out there among the abandoned farmland and Sandhills to this day, somehow, surviving without support from the rest of the world.

His first and last mistake was trusting them."

Morale

Morale is the CEO of a makeup company located in the Central Tower. She was infected with The Roots during the events of Lab Rat, leading to her becoming the second test subject in attempt to save her life. Her alien form is that of a flower. Morale possesses no mouth but still has a human like skull. Her tail is a balled up fire. Morale is part of the Gossip Girls as seen at the end of Lab Rat.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Green Ichor

Green Ichor was made by ANTIHUMAN Inc. as a experimental vaccine for The Roots. Anyone injected with the vaccine will go through a process called "Metamorphosis" turning them into an alien. Green Ichor could be an early version of the Fury mix seen in ANTIHUMAN and Y2K. The word "Ichor" means "the fluid that flows like blood in the veins of the gods.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Roots (virus)

Hcov-oc43E also known as "The Roots" is an airborne pathogen that is part of the coronavirus family. The Roots only infects humans showing up as black veins on the arms, chest, and neck before eventually attacking the heart and brain.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Central Tower

The Central Tower (CT) as the name implies is in the centre of Golden City, housing several different companies including ANTIHUMAN INC. The ring around CT is home to the Council. CT is alive and has drones as seen in Lab Rat. CT has colour recognition as it can recognize the blue of the CT uniform.

First Appearance: Y2K
