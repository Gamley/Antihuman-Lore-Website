Moka

Moka is a fluffy dragon alien with teal swirls on their cheek. They can also have visible scars on their chest. They are not from the Antihuman universe but ended up there after their spaceship malfunctioned and crashed in Golden City. Now they are looking for Ki and away back to their home universe, while investigating ANTIHUMAN INC.

First Appearance: ANTIVILLAIN

Gran

Gran is a fluffy dragon alien; almost identical to Moka with the exception of lacking scars on their chest and having greener eyes. They are Moka’s parallel in the Antihuman universe. They sometimes have Green "G"s on their cheeks. Gran is the founder and CEO of Antihuman INC and a member of the Golden City Council. Their goals are to stop "The Roots". They are a part of an alien religion.

First Appearance: ANTIHUMAN

Florret

Florret is the receptionist for Antihuman Inc. Her appearance is that of a slender golden tiger/bat hybrid with brown tiger markings and large bone "wings" which She uses like big and flexible hands. LovecutV has described Florret's sanity as a 1 out of 10, V has also stated that Florret had the "easiest" transformation out of the Gossip Girls. "Florret's the type to be a nudist frfr" - LoveCutV

First Appearance: The Ringmaster

Morale

Morale is the CEO of a makeup company located in the Central Tower. She was infected with The Roots during the events of Lab Rat, leading to her becoming the second test subject in attempt to save her life. Her alien form is that of a flower. Morale possesses no mouth but still has a human like skull. Her tail is a balled up fire. Morale is part of the Gossip Girls, as seen at the end of Lab Rat.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Vanilla Milkshake WHITE?

Vanilla Milkshake (commonly abbreviated to VM) is a transformed human in the form of a bat-like creature with orange eyes. They were an individual who became infected with The Roots virus, and in an attempt to save himself, he volunteered as the first subject of Green Ichor, Gran’s vaccine/cure. After knowingly risking and ultimately infecting the other human employees of Antihuman Inc, VM came out of the procedure as a failed test subjects due to his very poor mental condition post-transmutation, caused by his later stage of Roots. Lavender flowers have shown up in both music videos that VM has appeared in.

First Appearance: Data_Reject

Ki

Ki is a bat alien with a heart shaped cutout on her ear and has orange and yellow eyes (Heterochromia). She is roommates with Moka and was on their ship when it crashed in Golden City, disappearing afterwards. She is currently with the Gossip Girls. Ki is the Cosmic Critters universe parallel to Morale

First Appearance: N/A

Daryl Candi

Daryl Candi is the elected mayor of Golden City, his design is that of a pink sheep. He has been described as "nice and charismatic"

First Appearance: N/A

Cosmo

Cosmo is a news reporter for Channel 12 News, not much else is known about them.

First Appearance: The Ringmaster

Taw

Taw also known as Mr. Blue is an owl with multiple eyes. He is the one singing at the end of The Ringmaster (The blue text) and is a member of the Golden City Council.

First Appearance: The Ringmaster

Dandolin Candi

Dandolin is the daughter of Daryl Candi, the elected mayor of Golden City, and is part of a currently unknown alien species of elementals with cloud-like attributes. She appears visually similar to her father in physical features with the exception of her lack of pink body markings, cloven hands, singular horn and her preference of not manifesting legs and choosing to float on a column of pure cloud. Nothing is known of her mother as of yet. She has the ability to grow in size, as seen in a sketch from LovecutV, but chooses to remain small and condensed for personal reasons. Her larger size is reminiscent of a thunder cloud, with her pink hair turning a marbled grey with crackles of electricity. She is an employee of ANTIHUMAN INC and part of the Gossip Girls, as seen at the end of Lab Rat.

First Appearance: Lab Rat
